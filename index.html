What's happening in the modelling world? Who knows? We don't, but let's talk about it like experts. There's a rumor that Tamiya is making a 1:12 RB16B. Cool! Did you hear that Fujimi is reissuing all their '90s kits with itasha decal sets? Decidedly less cool! But now you know about it, at least.

Show off what you're working on! This is probably the most genuine part of the whole Klub. Scratchbuilding a spaceship from the You Extended Universe? Show it off. Building an overland Land Cruiser kitted out for intense mall crawling? Eh, no one cares but we'll try to be nice about it. No promises.

Why do you have so many unbuilt kits? Seriously, why? If you quit your job, and built one a month for the rest of your life, you'd have to live to be like fifty or sixty years old to build them all! Which isn't entirely unreasonable, but wait did you just buy another one while reading this? Who hurt you?
